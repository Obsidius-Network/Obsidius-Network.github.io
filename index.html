Bedwars

A popular minigame where players or teams protect their bed while trying to destroy the beds of their opponents. Once a bed is destroyed, the team can no longer respawn, adding high stakes to the competition. Obsidius Network's Bedwars comes with custom maps and unique gameplay tweaks to enhance the experience.

SMP

The SMP mode offers a classic Minecraft survival experience where players can gather resources, build structures, and collaborate or compete with others in a persistent world. The server enhances this mode with custom features to keep the gameplay fresh and exciting.

Lifesteal

In this competitive mode, players can steal health from their opponents by defeating them. This unique twist on survival gameplay adds an intense layer of strategy and challenge.

Custom Minigames

The server hosts a variety of custom-made minigames that you won't find anywhere else. These games are designed with exclusive plugins and mechanics to ensure that players have access to fresh and exciting content.

Custom Plugins

Obsidius Network utilizes a range of custom plugins that enhance gameplay, introduce new features, and ensure a smooth and unique experience. These plugins are tailored to create a distinct atmosphere and provide functionalities that standard Minecraft does not offer.

Custom Resource Pack

To complement the custom gameplay features, Obsidius Network uses a custom resource pack. This resource pack enhances the visual experience, providing unique textures and graphics that align with the server's themes and minigames.
